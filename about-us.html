Our Story

Founded on strong family values and a passion for quality construction, Royal-Roofing & Exteriors started with a simple mission: to provide reliable roofing services with integrity. What began as a small team with big goals has grown into a full-service roofing company trusted by hundreds of homeowners, business owners, and property managers across New Jersey.

Over the years, we’ve witnessed the impact that poor roofing workmanship and unreliable contractors can have on families and businesses. That’s why we made it our purpose to raise the standard—to be the roofing company people turn to when they want the job done right the first time.
